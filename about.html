About SN International Group

Remote support, matched with care.

We build dependable offshore support around the workflow founders and operators already run on — no template assistants, no faceless agencies, no surprises.

Our story

Started by operators, for operators.

SN International Group started with a simple frustration: the gap between what offshore support typically promises and what it actually delivers. The good talent existed — what was missing was the operating layer around it.

So we built one. We hire small, vet rigorously, and shape every engagement around the workflow our clients already run on. We pair every specialist with an SN account lead who's accountable for outcomes — not hours.

Today we support founders and operators across AU and US time zones, in four service tracks: Executive Assistance, Marketing Support, Content Creation, and AI Operations. Most clients start with one role and grow into a pod within six months.

Our promise is unchanged from day one: remote support teams, matched with care.

What we believe

The four ideas that shape every engagement.

Matched with care

No mass-shortlist tactics. Every match is hand-shaped to the brief, the workflow, and the communication style.

Outcomes over hours

We measure ourselves on what changes for the client — capacity, velocity, quality — not on timesheet padding.

Transparent by default

Fixed monthly fees. Scope in writing. Quarterly reviews. No mystery hours, no creeping mark-ups.

Human, every time

Tools and AI are amplifiers, not replacements. There's always a real person you can call by name.

How we hire

The top 1% — and the discipline to keep it that way.

A five-stage process designed to surface the rare combination of skill, judgement, and communication that real remote work demands.

01

Track-specific skills test

A short, role-relevant assessment. No trick questions, just real work.

02

Work sample

A take-home built around real client briefs. We mark for craft and judgement.

03

Communication interview

Async writing and a live call. Clarity, tone, and follow-through matter.

04

Culture & values screen

Reliability, ownership, and the bias-toward-action that makes remote work actually work.

05

Real work on a sample brief, paid at full rate. We don't ship people who haven't shipped for us first.
