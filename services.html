FAQ

Common questions

How fast can I get matched?

For most tracks, you'll meet 2–3 pre-vetted specialists within 24 hours of your discovery call. Typical launch is 7 days from brief.

What if it's not the right fit?

Every engagement is backed by our re-match guarantee. If a teammate isn't working out — first 90 days or anytime after — we re-match at no extra cost.

Can a single SN engagement cover more than one track?

Yes — most growing teams start with one track and expand. Pods coordinate across tracks under a single SN account lead.

How is pricing structured?

A simple, transparent monthly fee per role — based on track, seniority and hours per week. No recruitment fees, no mark-up surprises, no long lock-ins.

How do you handle data security?

Every team member signs an NDA and IP-assignment. We follow ISO-aligned access controls, password-manager standards, and on-request region-locked workstations.
